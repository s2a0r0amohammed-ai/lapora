Tips And Guidelines To Protect Your Laptop

1. Shut down regularly :

• Don't leave it running for days.

• Restart at least once a week to clear memory and install updates.

2. Keep It On a Hard And Flat Surface :

• Never use it on soft surfaces (bed, sofa, pillow).

• Blocked vents cause overheating.

3. Clean Dust From Vents Monthly :

• Use compressed air.

• Dust buildup kills cooling fans and reduces performance.

4. Avoid Liquids And Food Near The Laptop

• Spilled liquid can short-circuit the motherboard.

• Food crumbs damage keys.

5. Manage Battery Health :

• Keep charge between 20% and 80%.

• Don't leave it plugged in 24/7.

6. Update Software And Run Antivirus :

• Regular updates fix security holes.

• Scan for malware weekly.
